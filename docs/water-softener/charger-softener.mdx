## Quick Reference

**Program Parameters**

> System type: 7 alternating  
> Valve address: main is #1 and slave is #2  
> Regen type: meter immediate  
> Valve type: 2900/2910  
> Regeneration flow: down flow  
> Display format: US - gallons  
> Unit capacity: 240000 grains  
> Capacity safety factor: 00%  
> Feed water hardness: CITY WATER HARDNESS (GPG)  
> Regeneration day override: off  
> Regeneration time: 2:00 AM  
> Cycle 1 00: 10:00 backwash  
> Cycle 2 01:00: 00:00 brine & slow rinse  
> Cycle 3 00: 10:00 rapid rinse  
> Cycle 4 00: 14:00 brine tank fill  
> Cycle 5 off pause & delay  
> Auxiliary relay: disabled  
> Flow meter: generic  
> Maximum flow rate: 0150 GPM  
> Add 001 gallons every 020 pulses
