## Cheat Sheet

**SET Menu**

- ACC - Accel Time
- DEC - Decel Time
- LSP - Low Speed HZ (use for fixed HZ speed)
- HSP - High Speed (set to 60hz)
- ITH - Motor Amperage

**DRC Menu**

- BFR - Motor frequency (set to 60hz)
- UNS - Motor Voltage (460v)
- FRS - Motor frequency (set to 60hz)
- NCR - Motor Amperage -
